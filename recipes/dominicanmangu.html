Dominican Mangu

Smash plantains with cheese and salami.

Description:

The Dominican Mangu is a heavy breakfast meal made with smash plantains and os traditionally eaten with salami, fried cheese, eggs and slices of red onions. Trust me when I say it you have to try it.

Recipes details

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 25 mins
  • Total Time: 40 mins
  • Servings: 6
  • Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 3 green plantains
  • 1 quart water
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 1 ½ tablespoons salt

How to Make a Smash Burger

  1. Place the plantains and water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, and cook 20 minutes, until plantains are tender but slightly firm. Drain, reserving 1 cup of the liquid. Cool plantains, and peel.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and saute the onion until tender.
  3. Form the meat into loosely-packed balls.
  4. Place each ball on the grill, cover with a piece of parchment, and smash.
  5. In a bowl, mash the plantains with the reserved liquid and salt. Transfer to a food processor, mix in the peppers, and puree. Serve the pureed plantain mixture topped with the onions.
